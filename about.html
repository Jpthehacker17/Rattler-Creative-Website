ABOUT US

OUR TEACHER

Welcome! My name is Luis Ochoa and I teach in the Audio Visual Career cluster. I am a professional photographer, videographer, and graphic designer with over 8 years of industry experience. I have collaborated with companies of all sizes to expand their digital brand. I am honored to teach students at Sharyland High School the necessary skills for success in the media industry. In 2021, we started Rattler Creative, a media focused club that operates as the creative department for Sharyland High School. We look forward to growing and training students with hands-on experience in the media arts field.
