WE ARE

Rattler Creative is a student-led organization at Sharyland High School, dedicated to enhancing school spirit and involvement through multimedia projects. Founded on the principles of creativity and collaboration, Rattler Creative engages in a variety of activities including sports interviews, school club features, hype videos, short films, and photography. The club also excels in graphic design and actively participates in school events. Joining Rattler Creative offers a unique opportunity to develop your skills, work on exciting projects, and make a meaningful impact on school spirit and community.
